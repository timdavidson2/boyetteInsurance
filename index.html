Why an Independent Agent?

Not all insurance agents are the same! Choosing the right one can make a big difference – in price, service, and value.

With other agencies, you get one company that sells one brand of insurance. With an independent insurance agency like Boyette Insurance Agency you get choices.

Why? Because independent insurance agencies, like Boyette Insurance Agency, represent a number of different insurance companies, and can compare coverage and prices to find the best possible value for your individual circumstances. As independent agents, we are members of the Greater Richmond, Virginia community, and we’re committed to doing business face-to-face and being your advocate in times of need.

Here are a few reasons why:

  • We are not beholden to any one company. This means you don’t have to change agencies as your insurance and service needs change.
  • We offer one-stop shopping for a full range of products, including home, renters, auto, and business.
  • We can periodically review your coverage to keep up with your changing insurance needs.
  • We are committed to customer satisfaction – it’s the key to our livelihood.
  • We treat you like a person, not a number.
  • We are licensed professionals with strong customer and community ties.
